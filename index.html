My story...

I am based in Boston, Massachusetts. My journey into tech started with my side gig as a makeup artist. I wanted to create a website for my freelance makeup business and discovered how much I loved designing and developing websites. Now I get to combine my love of art, design, and tech into one.

My skills...

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer's PSD and quickly and accurately convert it into a webpage that is pixel-perfect match.
